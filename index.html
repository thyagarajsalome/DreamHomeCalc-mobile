Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate the cost of building a home? expand_more

Our app guides you through a step-by-step process. Simply input your plot size, desired construction area, material quality, and location to get a detailed cost estimate.

What factors affect the cost estimation? expand_more

Key factors include location (city-specific labor and material costs), quality of materials (basic, mid-range, luxury), total built-up area, and complexity of the design.

How accurate is the cost estimation? expand_more

Our estimates are based on current market rates in various Indian cities and are highly accurate for initial budgeting. For a precise quote, we recommend consulting with a registered contractor.
