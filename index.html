Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate the cost of building a home? expand_more

Our app guides you through a step-by-step process. Simply input your plot size, desired construction area, material quality, and location to get a detailed cost estimate.

What factors affect the cost estimation? expand_more

Key factors include location (city-specific labor and material costs), quality of materials (basic, mid-range, luxury), total built-up area, and complexity of the design.

How accurate is the cost estimation? expand_more

Our estimates are based on current market rates in various Indian cities and are highly accurate for initial budgeting. For a precise quote, we recommend consulting with a registered contractor.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

Welcome to DreamHomeCalc. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

DreamHomeCalc is a client-side application. This means all the data you enter—such as area, finish quality, and cost per square foot—is processed directly on your device.

  • We do not collect or store any personal data.
  • We do not transmit your calculations to any server.
  • All information remains private to you and is discarded when you close or refresh the app.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use it for any purpose.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not have any of your data to share. The "Share" functionality uses your device's native sharing capabilities to share an image of the results you have generated. We do not see or store what you share.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
